Rules: 

  A)basic

    1)Winner of two rounds wins match 

    2)Rock beat Scissor

    3)Paper beat Rock

    4)Scissor beat Rock


  B)play modes
    
   Night :

     1)don't need to select any card before playing

     2)can play any card

     3)if opponent is Sneaker then need to paly fist and reveal your card

     4)if opponent is Night then both cards are revealed at same time
   
   Sneaker :

     1)need to select 3 cards before playing

     2)can play from selected cards and played card is removed from selected cards

     3)if opponent is Night then need to paly after opponent revealed it's card 

     4)if opponent is Sneaker then both cards are revealed at same time
